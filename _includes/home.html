Who am I?

Hi there! I am a student at the University of Waterloo studying System Design Engineering. As an engineering student, I am excited to be learning a diverse variety of skills, from software development to solution architecture. My ultimate goal is to be developing solutions for problems that stretch beyond the scope of a traditional engineer and to help make the world a better place. I want to work with issues such as poverty, food security and public healthcare - but with a systematic and technical perspective. I love to meet new people and explore new things, so feel free to get in touch!
