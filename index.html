Electric Piano

This was a final project for a lab course I took in my first year of Engineering at Waterloo. It uses an FPGA board to control the frequency of two speakers by simply sending a binary signal at controlled time intervals. This oscillation of high and low then created some sweet tones of an electric piano.
Technology Used: Spartan 3 FPGA Board, Circuit Design, Verilog
